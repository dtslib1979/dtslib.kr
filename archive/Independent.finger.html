02 Tracks · Real Skill + Content OS
Two Tracks of Independence
The Independent always walks on two tracks: one physical survival skill, one content/AI operating system.
Real Skill Track (Hunting Skill) 3D / field / hands-on.
  • Think "work a robot will not bother to do".
  • Something useful in any country or city.
Content / AI OS Track How do they turn life into systems?
  • How do they capture experience as text, drawings, or code?
  • How will AI help compress and structure their story?
Real Skill = income + body + field.
Content OS = memory + structure + export.
